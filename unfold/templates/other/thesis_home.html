UnFold: A Modern Payment Platform for Publishers

By Sweyn Venderbush

Advisor: Ruzica Piskac

The internet has dramatically changed the way that people discover and consume media. The average consumer reads news a large number of articles from a wider variety of sources than ever before. But models of monetization haven't shifted to reflect this new paradigm of consumption, instead relying on the 20th century models of monthly subscriptions and advertisements.

This project aims to offer a proof of concept for a new model of monetization for web publishing. It allows publishers to price their content on a per article basis and gives users the ability to pay for articles for any in-network news source with minimal friction and no transaction costs via a centralized account, which also keeps track of every transaction and all the articles a user owns so they can easily come back and read them again.

The deliverable technical work consists of three pieces: a backend component that keeps track of user information and transactions on the network, a front-end component that allows publishers and users to manage their accounts and allows user to transact on the network, and a backend plugin for Django-based web applications as a proof of concept of how UnFold could be easily integrated into an existing publisher's site.
